Our story

We started Eserve Infotech to make software companies actually want to work with.

Most agencies treat clients like tickets. We don't. From the first call we behave like an internal team — short meetings, weekly demos, real ownership of outcomes.

We are headquartered in Croydon, serve clients across the UK and Europe, and lean on a global delivery network for senior engineering and design talent. Our work spans marketing sites, mobile apps, AI assistants, ERP/CRM systems and the SEO that makes them found.
