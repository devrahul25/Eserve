FAQ

Common questions about mobile app development.

Native or cross-platform?

Depends on your team and product. We share trade-offs honestly and let you choose. Most consumer apps land on React Native; high-perf apps go native.

Do you do design too?

Yes — most engagements include UX, motion and visual design. We also work alongside your existing design team.

How long to MVP?

Typically 8–14 weeks for a focused MVP, including backend and store submission.
